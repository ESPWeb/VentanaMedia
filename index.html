Frequently Asked Questions

  • ◆

    How do Customers Pay for the Service?

    We use PayPal Business. We send the customer a PayPal Business Link or a PayPal Invoice. Customers can then pay using their PayPal balance, a PayPal linked bank account, or a credit/debit card. A customer does not have to have a PayPal account to pay through PayPal with a credit/debit card.

  • ◆

    Can a customer receive an invoice?

    Yes, we can send customers a PayPal Invoice. A customer does not need to have PayPal to receive this invoice.

  • ◆

    Are there any ongoing cost?

    No, there are no monthly fees, no hosting fees, no domain fees (Unless you want to purchase your own domain name). We use Github (Owned by Microsoft) to host your website.
